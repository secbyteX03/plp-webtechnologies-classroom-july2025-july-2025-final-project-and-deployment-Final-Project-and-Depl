Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up to teach a skill?

+

Simply go to our Offer a Skill page and fill out the form with details about the skill you'd like to teach. Our team will review your submission and get back to you within 2 business days.

Is there a cost to join the platform?

+

No, joining and using Peerly is completely free! We believe in making skill-sharing accessible to everyone. You can browse skills, connect with peers, and share your knowledge without any charges.

How do I report inappropriate content?

+

If you come across any content that violates our community guidelines, please contact us immediately at report@peerly.com with details of the content you wish to report. Our moderation team will review it promptly.
